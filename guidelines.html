Operative Guidelines

To preserve the integrity of the Void, all operatives must comply with the following framework:

Originality of Content▸
  1. Open to operatives from all over the globe (all star).
  2. Operatives must use real-life public figures as face claims (FCs).
  3. Face claims must be aged 18–55. No minors, no zionists, no controversial figures.
  4. MV, OC, and AU roleplayers are welcome. No twins unless canon.
  5. One account per writer.
Profile Requirements▸
  1. New accounts are welcome. No minimum tweet requirement applies.
  2. Accounts must be publicly accessible throughout the Void.
  3. Basic visuals (profile picture and header) are required — no heavy edits necessary.
  4. Unique backstories aligned with each Void's theme are necessary (light guidelines will be provided per Void).
Language & Communication▸
  1. English must be used at all times for clarity and consistency.
  2. Uses of local memes and inside jokes are strictly prohibited.
  3. Light, respectful banter is allowed — keep IC conflict IC.
  4. Maintain contact with fellow operatives throughout your stay. Solitary silence may be interpreted as compromise.
Behaviour & Ethics▸
  1. Operatives are expected to uphold mutual respect. OOC disputes are not tolerated.
  2. No godmodding, metagaming, or hijacking another operative’s narrative.
  3. Any form of hatred, targeted harassment, or drama will lead to swift removal.
  4. Refrain from indulging in circle-in-circle behaviour. Please engage with all operatives fairly and equally.
Content & Timeline Etiquette▸
  1. NSFW (implied sexual, gore, graphic) content may not be posted after 00:00 GMT+7.
  2. Explicit content, especially of a pornographic or graphic nature, must not be liked, shared, or quoted onto the timeline under any circumstances.
  3. Maintain thematic integrity — preserve the illusion.
Activity & Participation▸
  1. Voids open periodically.
  2. Each Void runs for a set timeline unless extended.
  3. All operatives—regardless of account age—must remain active and fully committed throughout the Void.
  4. Daily operational minimum may vary depending on the nature of each Void.
  5. Inactivity for more than 24 hours without prior communication will result in removal and permanent suspension from future entries.
Admin & System Interaction▸
  1. Admins and bases operate under assigned codenames and may interact in-character.
  2. Admin support is always available for questions or clarifications — just transmit your inquiry.
